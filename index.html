About Us

Welcome to Queens Residences 3, a sanctuary nestled in the serene bayfront setting of Pulau Jerejak. This freehold development seamlessly blends modern living with the natural beauty of Penang's coastline.

Featuring 532 exclusive residential units, Queens Residences 3 offers five thoughtfully designed layouts ranging from 950 sq.ft. to 1,400 sq.ft., catering to diverse lifestyle needs. Whether you desire spacious units with panoramic sea views or cozy, functional spaces, there’s a home here tailored for you.

Enjoy breathtaking vistas of the sea and the iconic Penang Bridge from select units. Every detail has been meticulously crafted—from the strategic placement of facilities to open vistas—ensuring a seamless connection to the picturesque surroundings.

Embrace the tranquility of coastal living without compromising on contemporary comforts at Queens Residences 3.

Strategic Location

Conveniently located between the Queensbay Mall roundabout and the Gold Coast Condo roundabout, this prime spot ensures exceptional accessibility. Queensbay Mall is just a quick 2-minute drive away, offering a fantastic selection of shopping and dining experiences. For travelers, Penang International Airport is only 10 minutes away, providing effortless connections to both domestic and international destinations. Additionally, the Penang Second Bridge is easily accessible within a short 15-minute drive.
